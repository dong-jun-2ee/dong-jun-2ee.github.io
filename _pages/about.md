As a graduate researcher within the [MainLab](https://mainlab.skku.edu) at Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU), I am advised under [Prof. Jaekwang Kim](https://dramatic-samba-372.notion.site/Jaekwang-KIM-b5955ec7d50f488b8ff7bd29f9148641). My scholarly journey commenced in Mar. 2016 and culminated in Feb. 2022 with the attainment of a B.S. degree from Daegu Catholic University, South Korea. Throughout this period, my focus centered on the exploration and application of deep learning techniques to the processing and analysis of signal processing modalities, notably electroencephalography (EEG) signals.

Presently, my research endeavors are directed towards four primary fields: (1) recommendation systems, (2) generative models, (3) self-supervised learning, and (4) large language models. The crux of my work is the enhancement of user personalization and experience, driven by the ethos of `"Making the World a Better Place."` As I seek to achieve this goal, I am in pursuit of an opportunity with a forward-thinking organization where I can apply my expertise in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning to contribute to our collective future.
